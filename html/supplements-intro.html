Suppl.Preface:Supplements to A Course in Miracles

11The two supplements Psychotherapy: Purpose, Process, Practice and The Song of Prayer were scribed by Dr. Helen Schucman, as was A Course in Miracles, and are extensions of its principles.

21Psychotherapy, begun in 1973 and completed in 1975, offers a summary of the Course’s principles of healing: two people joined in sharing a common interest or goal. 2While ostensibly written for psychotherapists, all readers can benefit from its teachings.

31The Song of Prayer was scribed in 1977, and its three chapters summarize the Course’s teachings on prayer, forgiveness, and healing. 2It first presents the ego’s wrong-minded understanding of these terms, which is then contrasted with the Holy Spirit’s right-minded view.

41These supplements presuppose a basic understanding of the Course’s teachings, and are helpful adjuncts to a student’s study and practice of A Course in Miracles.
